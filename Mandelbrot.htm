Keyboard/Mouse Commands



Drag Mouse -- Zoom into selected region
Click -- Center view on clicked point

Right/Left Arrow Keys -- Compute with 10 more/less iterations
Up/Down -- Zoom into center of screen
Page Up --- Do twice as many iterations
C -- Change Coloring formula
I -- Change Coloring Scheme....Colors ounded solutions instead of unbounded (Can be much slower)
Z -- Open mouse zoom screen
